If your email provider supports plus addressing Mail Hash will append a hash to your email address:

your.name@gmail.com → your.name+h7so1@gmail.com

Providers that support plus addressing include gmail, FastMail, Mail.com, Outlook.com and many more

If your provide a custom domain Mail Hash will prepend a hash to your email address:

my-domain.com → ZpGM5rrfZ6@my-domain.com

You just need to set up catch-all/wildcard for your domain.
